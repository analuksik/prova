OUR FOUNDER, A TRUE LEADER, ALANAH RODRIGUEZ

Alanah Rodriguez is best known for saying: "Nobody leaves here without learning something new, everyday." Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Clear Glass, Rodriguez is a hands-on leader who continually helps to perfect Clear Glass’ products, improving transaction friction points, and providing the best rates for clients.

Rodriguez believes that by tracking and monitoring the right data, you can improve any business. "Having a team and 100 professionals also helps," she often adds.

Clear Glass improved scratch resistance by 40% in the last two years. They’ve also cut credit re-bills in half and switched to a new proprietary accounting system. She is proud of how their quality accounting is responsible for complementing the quality of Clear Glass’ products.
